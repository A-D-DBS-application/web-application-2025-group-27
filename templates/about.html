Our Mission

At Rival, we're an exceptionally driven team that got tired of watching companies spend weeks and outrageous consulting fees just to understand their own market. We knew there had to be a faster, sharper, truly modern alternative.

So we built Rival: a lightning-fast platform that enriches your company data and maps your competitive landscape with unmatched clarity. By combining industry data, team movements, funding activity, and powerful AI analysis, Rival helps you instantly spot relevant competitors and stay ahead of every shift.

We're aiming high: building the world's smartest market-intelligence engine and setting out to redefine how companies understand their markets.
